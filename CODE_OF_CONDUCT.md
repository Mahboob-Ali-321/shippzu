# 🌍 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Be respectful
- Be friendly
- Help others
- Accept constructive feedback

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment
- Hate speech
- Personal attacks
- Spam

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right to remove inappropriate content or contributions.
